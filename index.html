Frequently asked

Short answers to common questions. Still curious? Email us.

Is MamaSphere a medical device?

No. MamaSphere is a wellness tool. It helps you track, learn, and connect, but it does not diagnose or treat medical conditions. Always consult your healthcare provider for medical decisions.

How does the AI work?

We use Google Gemini AI for chat responses, report analysis, and insights. Every health recommendation in the AI chat includes citations to authoritative sources (ACOG, WHO, AAP, NHS UK, Mayo Clinic). Your data is not used to train AI models.

Is it free?

Yes, MamaSphere is free to download and use. Premium and Premium+ subscriptions unlock unlimited AI chats, more monthly report scans, ad-free experience, and additional Future Baby generations.

Do I need internet?

An internet connection is needed for AI features, community, and report scanning. Basic tracking (vitals, feeding, diapers, weight) works offline and syncs when you're back online.

Does it support premature babies?

Yes. Growth charts use corrected age automatically. There's a dedicated NICU support dashboard with phototherapy tracking, bilirubin monitoring, and NICU-specific community groups.

How do I contact support?

Email us at findaneasyway@gmail.com — we reply within 24 hours. You can also visit the Support page.
