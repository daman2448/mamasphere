1. Description of Service

MamaSphere is a maternal health companion app that provides health tracking, AI-powered insights, community support, and medical report analysis for users on their journey through trying to conceive (TTC), pregnancy, and postpartum care.

3. AI-Powered Features & Third-Party Data Sharing

MamaSphere uses artificial intelligence to provide personalized health guidance. By using AI features, you acknowledge and agree to the following:

3.1 What Data Is Shared

  • AI Health Assistant (Chat): Your chat messages, journey type (TTC/Pregnancy/Postpartum), and basic health context (e.g., pregnancy week, baby age) are sent to Google Gemini AI to generate responses.
  • Medical Report Analysis: When you upload medical reports for AI analysis, the report images or PDF files are sent to Google Gemini AI for processing.
  • Future Baby / Grow-Up Photos: Uploaded parent or baby photos are analyzed by Google Gemini AI to generate descriptions. Pollinations AI generates the resulting baby image.
  • Daily & Weekly Insights: Your tracked health data (vitals, mood, nutrition, feeding logs) is sent to Google Gemini AI to generate personalized insights and recommendations.

3.2 Who Receives Your Data

  • Google Gemini AI (operated by Google LLC) — processes chat messages, report analysis, photo descriptions, and health insights.
  • Pollinations AI — generates baby photo images based on text descriptions (no personal data is sent, only AI-generated text descriptions).
  • Firebase (operated by Google LLC) — stores your account data, health logs, chat history, and reports securely.
  • Google AdMob — displays advertisements to Free and Premium tier users. Ad-free for Premium+ subscribers.

3.3 How Your Data Is Protected

  • All data is transmitted over encrypted (TLS) connections.
  • Your data is not used to train AI models.
  • Your data is not sold to third parties.
  • You can delete all your data at any time from Settings > Delete Account.

3.4 Consent

The App will ask for your explicit consent before sending any personal data to AI services. You may decline, in which case AI-powered features will not be available.
