Frequently Asked Questions

How do I switch my journey (TTC / Pregnancy / Postpartum)?

Go to Profile > Switch Journey. Your previous data is preserved when you switch.

How does AI report scanning work?

Upload a photo or PDF of your medical report. Our AI analyzes it and provides a summary, findings, and recommendations. This is for informational purposes only — always discuss results with your doctor.

How do I generate a doctor report?

Go to Profile > Doctor Report. It automatically pulls all your tracked data for the period and generates a comprehensive PDF you can share with your healthcare provider.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your health data is stored securely and never shared with advertisers or third parties. You can delete your account and all data anytime from Settings.

How do I cancel my subscription?

iOS: Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > MamaSphere > Cancel
Android: Play Store > Profile > Payments & Subscriptions > MamaSphere > Cancel

How do I delete my account?

Go to Profile > Settings > Delete Account. This permanently removes all your data including reports, chat history, and tracking data.

Does MamaSphere support premature babies?

Yes. Growth charts use corrected age for premature babies, and there's a dedicated NICU support dashboard with phototherapy tracking and NICU-specific community groups.

Is the AI a replacement for my doctor?

No. MamaSphere's AI provides general health guidance for informational purposes only. Always consult your healthcare provider for medical decisions, diagnoses, and treatment.
