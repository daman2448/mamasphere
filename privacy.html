Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

MamaSphere ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and authentication credentials (via Google Sign-In, Apple Sign-In, or email/password).

Health & Tracking Data: Data you voluntarily enter including but not limited to: menstrual cycle data, pregnancy information, baby health metrics (weight, feeding, diapers, sleep), vitals (blood pressure, heart rate), mood, nutrition, supplements, and medical conditions.

Medical Reports: If you upload medical reports for AI analysis, the report images are stored securely in Firebase Storage and processed by Google's Gemini AI.

Usage Data: We collect analytics data about how you use the app (screens visited, features used, subscription status) through Firebase Analytics.

Device Information: Device type, operating system, and push notification tokens for sending reminders.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide personalized health tracking and insights based on your journey (TTC, pregnancy, postpartum)
  • To power AI features including the health assistant chat, report analysis, and personalized recommendations
  • To match you with relevant community groups based on your conditions and journey
  • To send reminders, notifications, and alerts you have configured
  • To generate doctor reports summarizing your tracked data
  • To display relevant product recommendations
  • To improve our app and services through anonymous analytics

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore, Firebase Storage, Firebase Authentication). All data is encrypted in transit using TLS and at rest. Profile photos are stored locally on your device only.

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, data storage, analytics, cloud functions, push notifications
  • Google Gemini AI: Powers AI chat responses and medical report analysis
  • Google AdMob: Displays advertisements to free and premium users (not Premium+ subscribers)
  • Amazon Affiliate: Product recommendation links — we earn a commission on purchases made through these links
  • Apple/Google: In-app purchase processing for subscriptions

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Your health data is never shared with advertisers. Anonymous, aggregated analytics may be used to improve our services.

Your Rights

  • Access: View all your data within the app at any time
  • Delete: Delete your entire account and all associated data from Settings > Delete Account. This permanently removes all your data from our servers.
  • Portability: Generate a comprehensive PDF report of all your tracked data via the Doctor Report feature

Children's Privacy

MamaSphere is not intended for use by children under 17. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Medical Disclaimer

MamaSphere provides health tracking and AI-powered guidance for informational purposes only. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical diagnoses, treatment, or advice. Always consult your healthcare provider for medical decisions.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or email.
